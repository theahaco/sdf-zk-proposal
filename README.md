## Two pillars

- **Pillar 1 — g2c**: ZK account recovery as a universal Soroban Smart Account primitive (BIP-39 seed + Poseidon2 commitment + 30-day time-locked rotation, plugged into OpenZeppelin's `do_check_auth` pipeline).
- **Pillar 2 — neftwerk**: ZK privacy circuits for the ARTS-1 token standard ("private title, public provenance"), with the same recovery primitive as Pillar 1.

Bundled because the two pillars share one substrate (Noir + UltraHonk + Poseidon2 + BN254 + Soroban 26.x). Funded as one $190k / 14-week grant with hard track separation between Pillar 1 and Pillar 2 disbursements.
